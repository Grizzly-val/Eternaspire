The two step inside. The doors close behind them with a thunderous echo.
Darkness. Then, a faint blue glow floods the hall.

CHALLENGER: …It’s colder than I thought.

NUGGET: That’s because this place isn’t alive. Not yet. The Tower watches every new challenger to see if they’re worth waking up for.

CHALLENGER: Great. I get judged by a building now.

NUGGET: (snickers) Oh, you’ll get used to it. The higher you climb, the more it notices you.

The floor pulses faintly, lighting a spiral pattern beneath their feet.

CHALLENGER: So this is where I start.

NUGGET: Yup! Floor Zero: “The Beginning of No End.” Kinda poetic, right?

CHALLENGER: (deadpan) Sounds like misery with extra steps.

NUGGET: (mock gasp) Don’t be so dramatic, merc boy! You’ve got me. You’ll be fine.

The camera lingers on Nugget’s grin — for a moment, her eyes flash with a faint, unreadable red hue.

NUGGET (softly, almost to herself): …You’ll be fine.
