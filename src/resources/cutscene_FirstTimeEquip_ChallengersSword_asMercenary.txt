NUGGET: That thing looks like it’s been through more fights than you.
CHALLENGER: (half-smile) Then it’ll feel right at home.
NUGGET: You’re serious about using that rust heap?
CHALLENGER: It’s not rust. It’s memory. You keep enough of it, and maybe it remembers how to win.
He swings once — slow, heavy — and the air ripples faintly, as though the Tower itself feels the motion.
NUGGET: (hovering closer) …That wasn’t normal. The Tower just blinked.
CHALLENGER: (tightens grip) Let it watch.
NUGGET: (forced laugh) Heh… sure! Nothing creepy about a sentient building staring at your sword!
The blade steadies in his hand, faint light tracing along the edge.
CHALLENGER: Every fight, every scar—it all leads here.
NUGGET: (quietly) Just don’t let it lead you.
